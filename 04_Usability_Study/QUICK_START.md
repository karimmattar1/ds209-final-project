## ðŸ“Š After All 3 Sessions - Create Final Report

### Step 1: List All Issues
Combine notes from all 3 participants. List every problem.

### Step 2: Count Frequency
- How many participants had this issue?
- 3/3 = Critical
- 2/3 = Important
- 1/3 = Consider

### Step 3: Categorize with MoSCoW

**MUST Fix:**
- Blocker issues (prevented task completion)
- Seen in 2-3 participants

**SHOULD Fix:**
- Major issues (made task very hard)
- Seen in 2-3 participants

**COULD Fix:**
- Minor issues
- Seen in 1 participant

**WON'T Fix:**
- Out of scope for this project

### Step 4: Write Solutions
For each MUST and SHOULD fix, write:
- **Problem:** What went wrong
- **Impact:** Why it matters
- **Solution:** How you'll fix it
