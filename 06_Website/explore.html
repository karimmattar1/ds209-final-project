Understanding the Metrics

  • Progressive Carries: Number of times a player moves the ball significantly closer to the opponent's goal
  • xG (Expected Goals): Quality of scoring chances created, based on historical data
  • xAG (Expected Assisted Goals): Quality of chances created for teammates
  • per 90: All metrics are normalized per 90 minutes played for fair comparison
